Critical Stages, Proven Protection: Scala® & Luna®
 

Your almonds face their greatest disease pressure during pink bud through bloom—the most critical stages for protecting your crop's potential. Scala®, Luna Sensation®, and Luna Experience® fungicides from Bayer deliver targeted disease management exactly when your orchards need it most.

 
Scala® at Pink Bud
 
Scala provides reliable early-season disease control when your buds are most vulnerable. It protects developing buds from fungal pressures, giving your crop a strong foundation for the season ahead.
 
Luna Sensation® Through Bloom
 
Luna Sensation's proven performance through bloom ensures continuous protection during this essential growth stage. Its broad-spectrum activity guards against disease development when your trees are actively flowering.
